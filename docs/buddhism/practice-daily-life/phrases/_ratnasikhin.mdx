### Ratnaśikhin Hṛdaya

<Phrase {...phrase} {...props} />

<details>
  <summary>
    Ratnaśikhin is a Buddha of the past who made the aspiration that anyone in
    the world-realms of the ten directions who hears his name at the moment of
    death will, after passing, be reborn as a [deva](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deva_(Buddhism))
    in [Trāyastriṃśa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tr%C4%81yastri%E1%B9%83%C5%9Ba).
    His vow is remembered as a source of comfort and confidence for those
    approaching death, offering a connection to virtue, clarity, and an
    auspicious rebirth.
