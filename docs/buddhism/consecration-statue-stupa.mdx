  <summary>
    Invocation and Request.
  </summary>
  <div>
    > All [Buddhas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buddhahood)
      and [Bodhisattvas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bodhisattva)
      abiding in the ten directions, please pay attention to us!<br />
    > Until all sentient beings, as infinite as the space itself,
      are brought to the state of perfect
      [nirvāṇa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nirvana_(Buddhism)) beyond extremes,
      may all the [victorious ones](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arihant_(Jainism))
      remain here constantly, without passing into
      [nirvāṇa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nirvana_(Buddhism))!<br />
    > In particular, may the [victorious ones](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arihant_(Jainism))
      remain here until the [four elements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mah%C4%81bh%C5%ABta#Buddhism)
      destroy these representations of enlightened
      [body, speech, and mind](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trikaya#Three_Vajras).<br />
    > And, as the [victorious ones](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arihant_(Jainism))
      remain here, please grant us and all other beings all the supreme
      and ordinary [siddhis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siddhi)!

  <summary>

  </summary>
  <div>
    > Like an assortment of many types of jewels,<br />
    > Set in the [Meru](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mount_Meru)
      of layers of exquisite refined gold,<br />
    > May these supreme holy bodily forms, that we can view insatiably,<br />
    > Remain firmly until the end of existence.

    > May there be the auspiciousness of the immutable-Meru holy body.<br />
    > May there be the auspiciousness of the sixty branches of holy speech.<br />
    > May there be the auspiciousness of the limitless ultimate holy mind.<br />
    > May there be the auspiciousness of the [Conqueror](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arihant_(Jainism))'s
      holy [body, speech and mind](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trikaya#Three_Vajras).
