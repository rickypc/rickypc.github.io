## Instructions

The video demonstrates [how to fill a Buddha statue](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iW2b-melDds). For deeper understanding,
refer to the [essential prayers for holy objects](https://fpmt.org/wp-content/uploads/teachers/zopa/advice/essential-mantras-for-holy-objects/essentialmantrasholyobjects_c5.pdf).

### Preparation

1. Bless all the [supplies](#supplies)

    This includes the statue or stupa itself and all the sacred items
    to be inserted.

2. Protect the face

    Wrap the statue's face gently in cotton wool to protect
    any painted features.

3. Clear the interior

    Use a screwdriver or similar tool to remove any leftover resin inside.
    - Unblock the neck
    - Smooth the hole
    - Break off or file down any small metal protrusions

    This helps reduce obstructions when inserting prayer rolls.

4. Clean the inside

    Wash the interior thoroughly with scented soap and water.
    Rinse well and allow it to dry completely.

5. Purify with smoke and seeds

    Burn Guggul resin underneath the statue or stupa while simultaneously
    throwing mustard seeds to dispel negativity.

6. Rinse with [saffron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saffron) water

    Pour [saffron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saffron) water inside and
    let it dry.

7. Paint and sprinkle sacred powders

    Paint the inside with eco-friendly red paint. While the paint is still wet,
    sprinkle:
    - Five metal powders
    - Gemstone powders
    - Medicinal, herbal, and incense powder

    Allow the paint to dry fully before proceeding.

### Filling Sequence

Insert the following sacred items and prayer rolls in the correct order
and placement:

1. Body section
    - <span style={{ color: 'gray' }}>White</span> pill
    - Loose [pearl](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pearl)
    - [Relics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%9Aar%C4%ABra)
    - Head prayer roll(s) (Buddha statue only)
    - <Link href="/pdf/panca-maha-suddhi.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pañca Mahā Śuddhi prayer roll">Five purification</Link> prayer roll(s)

2. Speech section
    - <span style={{ color: 'red' }}>Red</span> pill
    - Throat prayer roll(s) (Buddha statue only)
    - <Link href="/pdf/panca-maha-suddhi.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pañca Mahā Śuddhi prayer roll">Five purification</Link> prayer roll(s)

3. Life force pillar
    - Slide it in face-forward

4. Mind section
    - <span style={{ color: 'blue' }}>Blue</span> pill
    - Amṛta and Maṇi pills
    - Upper body, heart, and deity prayer rolls (Buddha statue only)
    - <Link href="/pdf/arya-tara-sataksara.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Āryatārā Śatākṣara prayer roll">100 syllables</Link> prayer roll(s)

5. Middle section
    - Amṛta and Maṇi pills
    - Rib and heart prayer rolls (Buddha statue only)
    - <Link href="/pdf/panca-maha-suddhi.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pañca Mahā Śuddhi prayer roll">Five purification</Link> prayer roll(s)

6. Lower section
    - Lower body prayer roll(s) (Buddha statue only)
    - <Link href="/pdf/pratityasamutpada.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pratītyasamutpāda prayer roll">Dependent origination</Link> prayer roll(s)

7. Final layer
    - Correction prayers
    - Various auspicious verses
    - <Link href="/pdf/panca-maha-suddhi.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pañca Mahā Śuddhi prayer roll">Five purification</Link> prayer roll(s)
    - <Link href="/pdf/sat-dharmakaya-sarirah.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Ṣáṭ Dharmakāya Śarīrāḥ prayer roll">Six dharmakāya relics</Link> prayer roll(s)
    - <Link href="/pdf/sapta-atitabuddha-karasaniya.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Sapta Atītabuddha Karasaniya prayer roll">Seven past Buddhas to eradicate sins</Link> prayer roll(s)
    - <Link href="/pdf/prajna-paramita.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Prajñāpāramitāhṛdaya prayer roll">Heart of the Perfection of Wisdom</Link> prayer roll(s)
    - <Link href="/pdf/vasuratna.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Vasuratna prayer roll">Wealth deity</Link> prayer roll(s)
    - Lotus prayer roll(s)

> Tip: After each layer, tightly pack medicinal, herbal, and incense powder
around the prayer rolls to fill all gaps.

### Final Additions

- Add [juniper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juniper) powder, five metal powders, gemstone powders, gemstones,
and five seeds powders.

- Place the [five Maṇḍala wheels](mandala-wheel.mdx):
  - [Jambhala](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jambhala) on top, facing downward (toward the statue's head or stupa's top)
  - Heads of the deities should face the back of the statue/stupa
    (as if they "stood up", they'd face the same direction as the statue/stupa)
  - [Viśvavajra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vajra) wheel at the bottom, facing downward toward the base

### Sealing

- Cover everything with a [<span style={{ color: '#ff9933' }}>saffron</span>](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saffron_(color)) cloth
- Close the opening with eco-friendly glue
- Seal the base with epoxy putty to prevent insects from entering

### For Small Statues or Stupas

If all prayer rolls cannot fit, use the <Link href="/pdf/sat-dharmakaya-sarirah.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Ṣáṭ Dharmakāya Śarīrāḥ prayer roll">six dharmakāya relics</Link> and
<Link href="/pdf/panca-maha-suddhi.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pañca Mahā Śuddhi prayer roll">five purification</Link> prayer rolls throughout.
Include as many <Link href="/pdf/sat-dharmakaya-sarirah.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Ṣáṭ Dharmakāya Śarīrāḥ prayer roll">six dharmakāya relics</Link> prayer rolls as possible.
