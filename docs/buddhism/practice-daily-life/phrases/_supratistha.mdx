### Supratiṣṭha

<Phrase
  instruction={(
    <>
      Perfect consecration mudrā:
      <ul>
        <li>
          The left hand is held above the items being blessed, palm facing
          downward, with the thumb pressing the little finger.
        </li>
        <li>
          The right hand is held below the items being blessed, palm facing
          upward, with the thumb pressing the little finger.
        </li>
      </ul>
    </>
  )}
  {...phrase}
  {...props}
/>

<details>
  <summary>
    A request for the [deities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buddhist_deities)
    and their retinues to remain present. In [Vajrayāna](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vajrayana)
    consecration rituals, this mantra is recited to invite the wisdom beings to
    approach a sacred object -such as a statue, stūpa, or painting- and to
    maintain a supportive presence. It creates a connection with their awakened
    qualities so the object can serve as a source of inspiration, protection,
    and practice.
  </summary>

</details>
