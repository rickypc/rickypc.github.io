### Mahāvairocana Sarvadurgati Pariśodhana Dhāraṇī

<Phrase {...phrase} {...props} />

<details>
  <summary>
    [Vairocana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vairocana) is seen as the [dharmakāya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dharmak%C4%81ya)
    (the supreme [buddha-body](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trikaya),
    the body of ultimate reality), and the embodiment of the Buddhist concept of
    wisdom and purity. In the conception of the [Five Jinas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Five_Tathagatas)
    of [Mahayana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mahayana) and [Vajrayana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vajrayana) Buddhism,
    Vairocana is at the centre and is often considered a [Primordial Buddha](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adi-Buddha).
    In East Asian esoteric Buddhism, Mahāvairocana is considered to be a cosmic
    Buddha whose body is the entire universe, the [Dharmadhātu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dharmadhatu).
  </summary>
