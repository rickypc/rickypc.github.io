### Pañca Mahā Śuddhi

<Phrase {...phrase} {...props} />

<details>
  <summary>
    The Five Great Purifications ([Chinese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters): 五大具威力的咒)
    are a set of powerful mantras used in various purification and consecration
    practices. They are traditionally listed as:
    <ol>
      <li>[Mahāvairocana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vairocana) ([Chinese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters): [毗盧遮那佛](https://zh.wikipedia.org/zh-cn/%E6%AF%97%E5%8D%A2%E9%81%AE%E9%82%A3%E4%BD%9B)) - [Sarvadurgatipariśodhana Dhāraṇī](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vairocana#Mantras_and_Dharanis)</li>
      <li>[Akṣobhya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akshobhya) ([Chinese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters): [阿閦佛](https://zh.wikipedia.org/zh-cn/%E9%98%BF%E9%96%A6%E4%BD%9B))</li>
      <li>[Uṣṇīṣavijayā](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Usnisavijaya) ([Chinese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters): [佛頂尊勝佛母](https://zh.wikipedia.org/zh-cn/%E5%B0%8A%E5%8B%9D%E4%BD%9B%E9%A0%82))</li>
      <li>Vimala Uṣṇīṣa ([Chinese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters): 無垢仏頂)</li>
      <li>[Amoghapāśa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Avalokite%C5%9Bvara#Manifestations) Padma Uṣṇīṣa ([Chinese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters): 白蓮花頂髻無垢密咒)</li>
    </ol>

    This set is distinct from the five great [dhāraṇīs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dharani)
    found in the [Six Dharmakāya Relics](index.mdx#ṣáṭ-dharmakāya-śarīrāḥ)
    collections, which form another traditional grouping used for consecration
    and blessing practices.
  </summary>
