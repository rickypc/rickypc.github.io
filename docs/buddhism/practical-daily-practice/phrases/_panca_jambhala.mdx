### Pañca Jambhala

<Phrase {...phrase} {...props} />

<details>
  <summary>
    The [five Jambhalas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jambhala#Five_Jambhalas)
    are the manifestations of the compassion of [Buddhas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buddhas)
    and [Bodhisattvas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bodhisattva) to guide
    [sentient beings](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sentient_beings_(Buddhism))
    along the path to [enlightenment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enlightenment_in_Buddhism).
    They have the essence of generosity and represent the activities of increasing benefit.
    Their aspirations are to help the poor and those suffering from ill-fortune.
  </summary>
