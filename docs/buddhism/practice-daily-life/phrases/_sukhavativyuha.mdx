### Sukhāvatīvyūha Dhāraṇī

<Phrase {...phrase} {...props} />

<details>
  <summary>
    This prayer's central purpose is to `pull out the fundamental cause of
    karmic obstacles`, purifying the roots of past actions and opening the way
    to liberation. Reciting it is traditionally believed to bring peace and
    clarity in this life, to purify accumulated karma, and to support rebirth
    in the highest level of [Sukhāvatī](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sukh%C4%81vat%C4%AB),
    the Pure Land of [Amitābha](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amit%C4%81bha).
    Known as the [Pure Land Rebirth Dhāraṇī](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amitabha_Pure_Land_Rebirth_Dharani)
    (往生咒), it is widely used in [East Asian Buddhism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_Asian_Buddhism)
    for oneself and for those who have passed away, helping to create
    auspicious conditions for release from suffering and for a favorable
    rebirth.
  </summary>
