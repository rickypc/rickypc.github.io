## Instructions

The video shows [how to fill Buddha statue](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iW2b-melDds).
The [essential prayers for holy objects](https://fpmt.org/wp-content/uploads/teachers/zopa/advice/essential-mantras-for-holy-objects/essentialmantrasholyobjects_c5.pdf) will explain further on the topic.

We bless all the [supplies](#supplies), including the statue/stupa itself.

Wrap the statue's face in cotton wool carefully to protect the painting.

We pick any leftover resin inside the statue/stupa with a screwdriver or similar tool.
We need to unblock the neck, smoothen the hole, break off, or file down any small
metal protrusions, to reduce the number of obstructions to put the prayer rolls inside.

We clean the inside of the statue/stupa thoroughly with nice scented soap and water,
rinse it well with water, and allow it to dry.

We burn Guggul resin underneath the statue/stupa and simultaneously throw
the mustard seeds to remove negativity.

Rinse the inside of the statue/stupa with [saffron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saffron) water,
and allow it to dry.

Paint the inside of the statue/stupa with a red color. While the paint is still wet,
sprinkle some five metal powder, gemstone powder, and medicinal, herbal & incense powder.

Allow the paint to dry before filling the statue/stupa.

Fill the statue/stupa by inserting the appropriate prayers and substances in the
correct order and in the correct places:
- Body (<span style={{ color: 'gray' }}>white</span>) pill,
  a loose [pearl](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pearl),
  [relics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C5%9Aar%C4%ABra),
  head prayer roll (Buddha statue only),
  and <Link href="/pdf/panca-maha-dharani.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pañca Mahā Dhāraṇī prayer roll">five purification</Link> prayer roll.
- Speech (<span style={{ color: 'red' }}>red</span>) pill,
  throat prayer roll (Buddha statue only),
  and <Link href="/pdf/panca-maha-dharani.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pañca Mahā Dhāraṇī prayer roll">five purification</Link> prayer roll.
- Slide in the life force pillar face forward.
- Mind (<span style={{ color: 'blue' }}>blue</span>), amṛta, and maṇi pills,
  upper body, heart, and deity prayer rolls (Buddha statue only),
  <Link href="/pdf/arya-tara-sataksara.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Ārya Tārā Śatākṣara prayer roll">100 syllables</Link> prayer roll.
- Amṛta and maṇi pills, rib and heart prayer rolls (Buddha statue only),
  and <Link href="/pdf/panca-maha-dharani.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pañca Mahā Dhāraṇī prayer roll">five purification</Link> prayer roll.
- Lower body prayer roll (Buddha statue only), and
  <Link href="/pdf/pratityasamutpada.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pratītyasamutpāda prayer roll">Dependent origination</Link> prayer roll.
- Correction, various auspiciousness verse,
  and <Link href="/pdf/panca-maha-dharani.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pañca Mahā Dhāraṇī prayer roll">five purification</Link> prayer rolls.
- Four Dharmakāya relic, stainless light,
  <Link href="/pdf/prajna-paramita.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Prajñāpāramitāhṛdaya prayer roll">Heart of the perfection wisdom</Link>,
  wealth deity, lotus prayer rolls.

After we fill each layer of the statue/stupa, we pack tightly medicinal,
herbal & incense powder amongst prayer rolls, filling every space.

We put [juniper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juniper) powder, five metal powder,
gemstone powder, gemstones, and five seeds powder.

Finally, place the [five maṇḍala wheels](mandala-wheel.mdx) with
[Jambhala](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jambhala) on the top
(towards the Buddha statue head or the top of the stupa) and the heads of the deities
directed towards the back of the statue/stupa.
The [Viśvavajra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vajra) faces down toward
the base of the statue/stupa like [Jambhala](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jambhala).
(so if they "stood up", they would face the same direction as the statue/stupa).

Cover everything with a [<span style={{ color: '#ff9933' }}>saffron</span>](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saffron_(color)) cloth,
then close with eco-friendly glue, and seal the base of the statue/stupa with epoxy putty,
so that no insects can gain access.

When filling a small statue/stupa wherein all the listed prayer rolls will not fit,
the four Dharmakāya relic and
the <Link href="/pdf/panca-maha-dharani.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="Open Pañca Mahā Dhāraṇī prayer roll">five purification</Link> prayer rolls
can be used throughout.
The four Dharmakāya relic prayer rolls to be used as much as possible.
