## Sacred Phrases for Devotional Practice

This section collects all the sacred phrases I've learned over the years.

> I use the word **"phrase"** to describe what is often known as a **mantra** -
> a short, complete saying used in devotional practice.
> For example, <code>{aryaTaraPhrase.transliteration.children}</code> is a
> single phrase directed to {aryaTaraPhrase.transliteration.title.replace(/ Hṛdaya/, '')}.

> Rather than using the word **"prayer"**, which can sometimes suggest a full
> ritual sequence or structured worship service, I focus here on the spoken
> invocations themselves - the concise expressions of devotion, intention, and
> reverence.

Each phrase listed here is a distinct spiritual utterance, often associated
with a particular deity, purpose, or blessing. Whether used on its own or as
part of a larger practice, each one stands as a meaningful and complete act of
connection.
