### Śākyamuni Hṛdaya

<Phrase {...phrase} {...props} />

<details>
  <summary>
    Through simply hearing this sacred heart-essence of Buddha [Śākyamuni](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Buddha#Siddh%C4%81rtha_Gautama_and_Buddha_Shakyamuni)
    (the Munīndra Hṛdaya Mantraḥ), one is said to gather vast merit and purify
    karmic obscurations. When recited, it protects the practitioner from
    obstacles and supports the unfolding of wisdom. These qualities are taught
    in the abridged [Prajñāpāramitā](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prajnaparamita),
    where this mantra is praised as the essence of the Buddha's liberating
    insight.
  </summary>
  <div>
